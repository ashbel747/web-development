FAQs

Find answers to your questions about our plant-based snacks and their benefits.

What are PlantFuel snacks?

PlantFuel snacks are high-protein, plant-based options made from lentils and quinoa. They are designed for athletes and active individuals seeking nutritious, convenient snacks. Our products contain no additives or preservatives, ensuring a clean eating experience.

Are they gluten-free?

Yes, all PlantFuel snacks are gluten-free. We prioritize ingredient transparency and ensure our products meet dietary needs. You can enjoy our snacks without worrying about gluten.

How are they packaged?

Our snacks are packaged in eco-friendly materials to minimize environmental impact. We believe sustainability is key, and our packaging reflects that commitment. Enjoy your snacks while caring for the planet.

What flavors are available?

We offer a variety of delicious flavors to cater to different taste preferences. From savory to sweet, there's something for everyone. Check our product line for the latest offerings.

How can I order?

You can order directly from our website once we launch. Sign up for early access to stay updated on product availability. We’ll make the ordering process simple and convenient.
